1. Introduction
------------------------------------------------------------
Earth Systems Stabilization is the planetary-scale benefit that emerges when sufficiently many communities deploy TESS-128 arrays, AQUEM-based behaviors, and local microclimate harmonization.

This is *not* geoengineering.  
It does not inject aerosols, alter atmospheric chemistry, or force climate outcomes.

Instead, Cosmic Peace stabilizes Earth by **reducing local volatility**, which allows natural systems to self-correct, self-balance, and regain the rhythmic regularity they had before industrial and climatic disruption.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. Stabilization vs. Control
------------------------------------------------------------
Cosmic Peace does *not* attempt to control global climate.

It:
- smooths local extremes  
- prevents runaway feedback loops  
- buffers stress points  
- enables natural atmospheric flow  
- keeps systems within safe operational ranges  

This approach is analogous to:
- reducing vibration in machinery so the machine runs smoothly  
- reducing turbulence in water so the river flows naturally  
- moderating heat spikes so ecosystems don’t collapse  

Earth stabilizes itself once shocks are removed.
